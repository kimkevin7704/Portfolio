Hi there!

My name is Kevin Kim and I'm an aspiring game developer, software engineer, musician, and orca enthusiast.

In 2010 after graduating with a degree in Biology, I realized my true passion was in gaming. I was in the intense business of slaying nerds and stealing ladder points until I realized I wanted to build dope stuff. In 2012, I purchased an Oculus Rift for development purposes and moved to Texas to pursue an education in Computer Science. After finding other students who shared the same passion for virtual/augmented reality that I had, I founded the Virtual Reality Development Group and began working at the Virtual Reality Technology Lab at Texas State University. Now, as a recently graduated software engineer, I am continuing my pursuit towards glorious ownage as a game developer.

Currently, I am working on personal projects and pursuing my next venture! Life's a trip and I'm stoked for whats next!
